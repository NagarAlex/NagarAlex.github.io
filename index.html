Research Interests

These are the keywords (as is customary) that summarize my research interests: Economic History, Institutions, Technical Change, Agriculture, Industrialization (or lack thereof) in the "Periphery", Traditional Industries, Monetary History.

My DPhil work has focused on the Indian subcontinent, but I'm also interested in the rest of Asia. I am not so sure about many of the usual explanations as to why growth in Asia (except Japan) was so low until the 1980s.
